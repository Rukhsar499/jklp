About Programme

Eligibility: Class 12 Any Stream with Minimum 45% Marks

B.Sc. (Artificial Intelligence) with Animation & VFX is a 4-year undergraduate program designed to equip students with advanced knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, integrated with creative applications in Animation, Visual Effects, AR/VR, and Multimedia production. The course aims to produce AI-enabled creative professionals who can work in the rapidly expanding AVGC (Animation, VFX, Gaming, Comics) and digital industries. The program is designed to:
